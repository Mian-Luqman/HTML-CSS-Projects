Who we are…

Tasty Bakery Cafe’s traditions have been passed down for decades of generations. With over 20 years of baking experience, the family business was designed in a modern Brazilian style to give customers a genuine Brazilian experience. Tasty Bakery Café provides a wide array of authentic Brazilian specialty food items such as baked goods, pastries, desserts, sandwiches, smoothies, and Brazilian blend coffee.

Our baked goods, which include cakes, sweet rolls, croissants, breads, and cookies, are accessible on a self-serve basis. Providing our customers in the surrounding Marietta, Kennesaw, Woodstock, Canton, and Acworth area expedited service with an excellent customer experience.

Our mission is to provide delicious and authentic Brazilian baked goods, pastries, and sandwiches while maintaining quality as the number one ingredient in everything that we do.
